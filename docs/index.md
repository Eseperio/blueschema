# BlueSchema: Bridging AI and Human Understanding

## The Only Schema Language for Collaborative AI Development

BlueSchema is **the revolutionary way to track and define applications** in a format that both AI and humans can understand and share. In today's AI-driven development landscape, we need a common language‚Äîa shared context that eliminates ambiguity and enables seamless collaboration between human developers and AI tools.

### Why BlueSchema Matters

Modern software development faces a critical challenge: **AI tools and human developers don't share the same context**. When an AI generates code, it often lacks understanding of your application's structure, relationships, and business logic. When humans review AI-generated code, they struggle to ensure consistency across an entire application.

**BlueSchema solves this problem** by providing:

## How It Works

BlueSchema uses **JSON Schemas** to describe every aspect of your application:

1. **Entities and Data Models** - Define your data structures with fields, types, relationships, and constraints
2. **Actions and Business Logic** - Describe operations with prompts that guide AI code generation
3. **Services and Integrations** - Specify external services and their behaviors
4. **Access Control** - Model roles, permissions, and security requirements
5. **UI Components** - Layout views and interfaces with declarative schemas

Each component includes rich descriptions that act as **prompts for AI tools**, ensuring generated code matches your intent precisely.

## Explore the Schemas

BlueSchema provides two main categories of schemas:

### Application Flow Schemas
Define your application's logic, data, and behavior:

- [**Application**](/blueschema/schemas/application) - Root schema that ties everything together
- [**Entity**](/blueschema/schemas/entity) - Data models and structures
- [**Field**](/blueschema/schemas/field) - Field properties, types, and constraints
- [**Relation**](/blueschema/schemas/relation) - Entity relationships
- [**Action**](/blueschema/schemas/action) - Business logic operations
- [**Group**](/blueschema/schemas/group) - Action organization
- [**Service**](/blueschema/schemas/service) - External service integrations
